About PGGMB School Sungai Akar

Sekolah PGGMB Sungai Akar (PGGMB School Sungai Akar) is a private primary and kindergarten school located in Simpang 396, Kampong Sungai Akar, Jalan Kebangsaan Lama, Berakas, Bandar Seri Begawan, Brunei Darussalam. The school operates under the approval and recognition of the Ministry of Education, Brunei Darussalam.

Established with the aim of providing quality education in a caring and supportive environment, PGGMB School Sungai Akar is committed to nurturing students who are academically capable, morally upright, and socially responsible. We believe that every child has unique potential, and our role is to guide and inspire them to become confident lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be a School of Choice that provides holistic education, producing knowledgeable, skilled, and morally grounded students who are prepared to face future challenges.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education supported by knowledgeable, caring, and approachable mentors.
  • To foster a passion for learning and strong moral values among students.
  • To create a safe, positive, and inclusive school environment.
  • To encourage continuous learning and personal growth.
  • To work in partnership with parents and the community.

Our Core Values

  • Respect
  • Responsibility
  • Integrity
  • Excellence
  • Compassion

Our Approach

At PGGMB School Sungai Akar, we adopt a balanced approach to education that integrates academic learning with character development. Our curriculum is designed to meet national education standards while incorporating engaging teaching methods, practical activities, and the use of modern technology to enhance learning experiences.

We place strong emphasis on discipline, respect, and Islamic values, ensuring that students grow not only intellectually but also spiritually and emotionally.
