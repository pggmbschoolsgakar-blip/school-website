Welcome to PGGMB School Sungai Akar

PGGMB School Sungai Akar is a private kindergarten and primary school committed to providing quality education in a caring, supportive, and values-based environment.

Guided by our mission to foster a passion for learning and strong moral values, our school strives to be a School of Choice through continuous improvement, dedicated teachers, and meaningful partnerships with parents and the community.

Our Vision

To be a School of Choice that provides holistic education.

Our Mission

To provide quality education supported by caring mentors.

Our Values

Respect, Responsibility, Integrity, Excellence, Compassion.
